CUT TO:

INT. LORGE’S APARTMENT - NIGHT

The apartment is decorated with rocks of all shapes and sizes. Gorge, Larry, and Gessica stand around awkwardly, holding drinks and trying to mingle with the other guests.

LORGE walks in with Rocky perched on her shoulder.

LORGE: (to the guests) Thank you all for coming to Rocky’s party! He’s so excited to be here tonight.

The guests stare at her in confusion.

Lorge and her friends laugh and continue to enjoy the rest of the party.
